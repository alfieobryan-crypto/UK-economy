About this data: Revenue figures are HM Treasury / OBR forecasts from the Autumn Budget 2024. Total Managed Expenditure (£1,237bn) is higher than the departmental spending shown on the Spending page (£977bn) because it includes all local government spending, capital investment, and some items not allocated to specific departments. PSNB (Public Sector Net Borrowing) is the standard measure of the annual deficit — it excludes financial transactions such as bank bailouts. National debt figures are for public sector net debt excluding Bank of England.

Data sourced from HM Treasury · OBR Autumn Budget 2024
